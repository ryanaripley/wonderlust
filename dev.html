Come play in our world for a night.

What is it?

Wonderlust. The desire for that which stops us in our tracks. The hunger for that quickening of the heart, that welling-up, that wide-eyed awe. The itch that only art can scratch.

When we find it, for a moment we are startled into clarity. For a moment we transcend. That is why we crave it, and why there is such power in creation.

On May 15 join us for a benefit to honor playwrights: the creators, the world-builders and spell-casters, the insatiable makers, the architects of awe who take us to a hidden place beyond the day to day. Raise a glass to the artists who devote their lives to the quest for the profound.

Shed the scaffolding of your everyday routine and plunge into the (im)possible, the (un)imaginable, the new and never-been. Satiate your cravings for the “Wow!”, the “Oh!” and the “Yes!”. Above all, check your dull at the door.

Meet and mingle with the country’s hottest playwrights, actors, directors and leaders from across the country, and see up to six productions of new plays by the nation’s most exciting playwrights all in the course of one thrilling weekend! This package includes weekend passes to the Festival (valued at over $770), roundtrip airfare for two, and a two-night stay at a Marriott hotel.

    About The Curry Diva: Over the last 30 years, Heather Jansz has launched Sri Lankan restaurants in Minneapolis and St. Paul and cooked for everyone from Deepak Chopra to Black Sabbath. She is credited with bringing curry to Minnesota, and as one fan said, “she makes magic edible.”
